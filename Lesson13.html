Assignment

For the final project I am going to build my own 3D-Printer from scratch.
The printer should have a big printing area (400*400)
It should print PLA, therefore I do not necessarily need a heated bed
The printer should been build on low budget, so I want to print many parts on my own, all other parts can be seen in the Bill of materials

How did what beforehand?

Since the RepRap community has started, a lot of open source printers were made
There are a lot of creative and nice projects which could be included in my printer as well
Still I do not have a lot of experience, so I want to first build the printer itself and then upgrade it with a few special tools
here you get a few impressions:

A printer which can move its head to quantify the z-axis

Printing with one or more colors
